About TableTrove


TableTrove was created by Thomas Garbe O'Connor.


Description


TableTrove is a website for people to create and share their ideas for items and creatures. While TableTrove aims to be a tool for Dungeons & Dragons 5e, it can realistically be used for any TTRPG. On the homepage you can search for previous creations by the community (exact matches only). You can also find the links to item and creature creation. Lastly, the home page shows off the top-liked item and creature.

Both items and creatures can be viewed on their own pages which lists information about them, including a link to who created the post as well as a thread of comments. Here you can leave a comment or like the item if you are logged in.

When you navigate to a user's details page, you can see their name, profile picture (if it exists), distribution of posts through a pie chart, and all of their previous posts.

TableTrove features secure user management and source management.
